Computer Science Engineering ~ CSE
About

To provide necessary strength to enable the students to innovate and become successful professionals. The dept. is offering under graduate (BE) in CSE with its cohesive team of faculty members with knowledge of latest technology offers a sound program at the U.G. level. The dept. right from it's inception in 2000, has gained the recognize by the reputed S/W companies all over the country. The curriculum is blend of the conventional, the critical and practical oriented courseware to combat with growing demand and the changing thread of the S/W industries and wide application of computers at every step of our life.Core courses include programming languages , Computer Architecture, Microprocessor & Interfaces, System Software, N/W Technologies & Applications.

Message from HOD

The Bachelor of Engineering degree in Computer Science and Engineering is accredited by AICTE . The Computer Science and Engineering provides students with knowledge of basic and advanced topics in all areas of computer science and engineering like Data Structures, theory of Computation, Java programming and Grid Computing. The students are given through training on various Laboratories to have hands on experience. In computer science you will study and apply your knowledge in understanding what computers are, as how to program them, tools to write a program, the rules when converting the written program understandable by the computers, the interface between the computer and the user, the computer graphics, computer networking, managing the software database, software engineering and testing them efficiently and more. Now computers are used everywhere to reduce the manpower requirements, easy of computation and to improve the efficiency. So the scope for computer science is endless.

Mechanical Engineering ~ ME
About Department of Mechanical Engineering offers 4 years B.E. Degree in Mechanical Engineering. The Department strives to impart quality education keeping in view the global scenario and social obligation. The track record of the department, judged by the employment potential of our students and noteworthy achievements of illustrious alumni, by any stands, is excellent. The department strives for continuous improvement in quality of education through curriculum development and up gradation of laboratories. The department could boast of by establishing very well equipped CAD, Heat Engines & Strength of Materials, Refrigeration and Air Conditioning, Hydraulics & Theory of Machines Laboratories. The department is also going to establish a well-equipped workshop that caters to the need of the entire college.

Message from HOD

Our Mechanical Engineering branch is one of the best equipped with the most modern machines and equipment in the region. Our workshop ensures practical machining on sophisticated metal cutting machines along with Welding Shop, Carpentry Shop, Fitting Shop, Sheet Metal Shop, Foundry&Forging Shops. Students go for practical experiments in laboratories like Basics of Mechanical Engineering Lab, Strength of Material Lab, CAD Lab, Fluid Mechanics, Fluid Machines Lab, Energy Conversion Lab, IC Engine Lab, KOM & DOM Lab, Heat Transfer Lab,RAC Lab, Automobile Engineering Lab etc. The department has also state-of-the-art equipments in the workshop and various labs such as material science, measurement & metrology, kinematics, dynamics of machines, vibrations, refrigeration & air conditioning, heat and mass transfer etc. In these laboratories students perform experiments as per syllabus and beyond to gain knowledge and expertise. Civil Engineering
About

The Civil Engineering branch was established in the year 2015 (current session). The department offers semester based Four years B.E. Degree course. This is approved by AICTE (New Delhi), DTE (Raipur) and CSVTU (Bhilai). The total intake in this branch is 60. The motto of the department is to provide technical excellency in the field of Civil Engineering. All the facilities for practical are available for students. In near future the department will be upgraded with all the laboratories to fulfill the requirement of BE (Civil Engineering) student.

Message From HOD

Today’s civil engineer should possess in depth Civil Engineering skills for analysis, design, construction and maintenance of various structures, computer, managerial and communication skills for better management of various structures. He should be also trained to understand and solve real world problems in Civil Engineering. There are many career paths for civil engineers. Civil engineers can work in Government departments (ranging from municipality, corporation, state and central governments and defence), public sector undertakings like SAIL, GAIL, NTPC, NHPC, BHEL etc. and various private sector organizations (contracting and consulting firms). Initially, the nature of job may be construction supervision, design, marketing or production. As they gain more experience, they manage projects. Mr. Sanjay Kumar Mirania HOD CIVIL

Electrical Engineering
About

The Electrical Engineering branch was established in the year 2015 (current session). The department offers semester based Four years B.E. Degree course. This is approved by AICTE (New Delhi), DTE (Raipur) and CSVTU (Bhilai). The total intake in this branch is 60. The motto of the department is to provide technical excellency in the field of Electrical Engineering, Electronics Engineering, Digital Electronics & Microprocessor. This department deals/teach the subject Element of Electrical Engineering (EEE) for the first year all branches students, as per syllabus of CSVTU (Bhilai). Basic Electrical Engineering lab has been setup as per the requirement of the syllabus for first year students; All the facilities for practical are available for students. In near future the department will be upgraded with all the laboratories to fulfill the requirement of BE (Electrical Engineering) student.

Message from HOD

With the growing demand of Electrical Engineers in the Government and private sectors, the Department is making best efforts to produce highly trained and capable engineers who can take up the challenges of the real world. The quality of academic instructions, conduct guidelines and college activities are designed to produce competent and successful engineers. In the Department, the focus is on preparing professional engineers.As far as the Department is concerned, academic standards and practical work are the two key parameters. The Department of EE has a fine blend of renowned as well as young and dynamic personalities as faculty, who are involved in imparting quality education. The faculty strives to foster and encourage a teaching methodology that is both practical and theoretical in approach. This orientation has led to successful projects and trainings.
