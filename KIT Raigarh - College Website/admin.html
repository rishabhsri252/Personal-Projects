About KIT Raigarh

Kirodimal Institute of Technology, first Engineering college of Raigarh was established in 2000. It is governed under the Kirodimal Polytechnic Society and affiliated to Chhattisgarh Swami Vivekanand Technical University (CSVTU), Bhilai (CG) and is approved by AICTE, New Delhi. KIT is committed towards forging students to become globally acceptable technocrats by virtue of their sheer hard work, proper training in various fields and inculcating human values in them. Institute is located at Garhumaria, Odisha Road, Raigarh (CG). It is well connected by National highway, South eastern railway. Key initiative like teacher guardian scheme, book bank scheme, induction of resource books, and yearly organisation of events like technical and cultural festivals makes K.I.T an institute with a difference. Thus, within just a few years of its existence, K.I.T has carved out a niche for itself as one of the leading engineering colleges under the C.S.V.T.U., Bhilai (CG).

Vision

The vision of K.I.T is to establishing unique identity by development of high quality human and knowledge resources in diverse areas of technologies to meet local, national, and global economic and social need and human society at large in self-sustained manner. And to create academic center par excellence, fulfilling the needs, aspirations and expectations of society through Mission technological innovations.
